#{s_('river-tips|Tips:')}

  • #{_('For the search, enter one or more words or hashtags, separated by spaces.')}
  • #{_('Posts having any of the words will be included in the river.')}
  • #{_('Use a minus sign (-) in front of a word to exclude posts with that word.')} #{_('Example: %s#music -#classical%s will find posts about music, but not classical music.') % ["", ""]}
  • #{_('Use a plus sign (+) in front of a word to include only posts with that word.')}
  • #{_('Use the special term %s to include only posts which you have not read yet.') % "+:unread"}
  • #{_('Use the special term %s to include only posts by members of the same tree as you.') % "+:tree"}
  • #{_('Use double quotes to match multi-word phrases, or exact matches involving symbols.')}
  • #{_('Use the special term %s followed by a JID in quotes to match all posts by that person.') % ":from"} #{_('Example: %s') % ':from "rekado@oak.elephly.net"'}
  • #{_('Use the special term %s followed by a contact list name in quotes to match all posts by the people in that contact list.') % ":contact-list"} #{_('Example: %s') % ':contact-list "Close Friends"'}
  • #{_('Use the special term %s followed by %s or %s to match posts with a given visibility.') % [ ":visibility", "internet", "forest", ]}
  • #{_('Other special terms: %s, %s and %s. These are for matching posts you have liked, commented on and subscribed to, respectively.') % [":liked", ":commented", ":subscribed"]}
  • #{_('(advanced) Use the special term %s followed by a source name in quotes to match posts posted with a given source or tool.') % [ ":via" ]}
  • #{_('(advanced) Use the special term %s followed by a river label in quotes to compose rivers using other rivers.') % ":river"} #{_('Example: %s:river "Special Interests"%s') % ['', '']}
  • #{_('(advanced) Use the special term %s followed by < or > and a number to match posts by their approximate number of words.') % ":word-count"} #{_('Example: %s:word-count < 16%s') % ['', '']}
